Day 1
Stress from situation back home.
Brain feels foggy.
Beautiful clouds [plane] flight.
Amazed by buildings esp [especially] the reception.
Interesting stories by concierge.
Casserole & Steak Ale Pie at bar.
Beer, guy next to me staring at me.
Tired, back to room, sleep.

Day 2
Skin situation, go out to get products, take long. Belinda waits.
Walk to town.
Belinda amazed with architecture.
We walk up to the castle.
Statue with Zulu War 1900.
JK Rowlings Handprints.
Outsiders Lunch, Yum!
Walk in Holyrood Gardens, I see the castle.
Gen-Z's comment: "Castle's only for rich people".
On the hunt for Unicorn.
Josh's Bar, pub snacks & flat cider.
Holy Mile.
Early Dinner at Fava.
On route home Belinda discover Tesco Biltong.

Day 3
Run to Cramond, Happy Labrador (14km).
Walk along River Leith.
All the way past sport fields to Stockbridge.
Back hotel room, chill, beer.
Patatino, Huxton Whiskey, Hotel Bar.

Day 4
Walk to Arthur's Seat, in normal clothes, thanks Belinda.
"Hike" with All Stars, "formal" pants.
Nice views, rush back down to be on time for Mary's Close.
Exactly on time 13:00.
Stephon aka Steve.
Josh our very energetic fast talking guide.
Steve is going to hate Josh, I don't hate him just wish I could understand him.
"Green Grim Reaper" Annie Room.
The door behind Josh, Steve's reply: "Behind You".
Love the history but I was hoping it would be Mary Queen of Scots.
Drink at oldest pub White Inn.
Stout good.
Rockin Rudolph terrible beer, tell "everyone" in bar.
No food, drunk, go "Home" aka Hotel.
On way home hungry Belinda, Steve knows the area & knows good take away comes.
Belinda hungry is like a mirage Aboosh appears (Show photo of Belinda).
She phoning Jaynie & telling the Josh story.
Aboosh unhealthy but so good, end up throwing away.
Did we spoil our appetite?
Taxi driver taking personal call, window open, lost cat? lost bird?
The evening our big last meal at The Witchery.
Weird crowd on street, jongerns, I am actually kind of scared of them.
Tired, home, sleep.

Day 5
Supposed to go to Holyrood Castle, decide not to because we experienced the stress with taxi & decide to take it easy.

Breakfast & go to airport.
